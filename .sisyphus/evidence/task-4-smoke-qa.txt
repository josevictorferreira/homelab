=== Root Cause Analysis ===

The `cliTools` buildEnv (defined in oci-images/openclaw-nix/default.nix) contains:
- pkgs.chromium
- pkgs.fontconfig  
- pkgs.dejavu_fonts
- pkgs.noto-fonts
- etc.

However, these packages are NOT being included in the final image. The issue
appears to be with how `streamLayeredImage` handles the `buildEnv` closure.

In `extraCommands`, there's a loop that should symlink `${cliTools}/bin/*` into
`./bin/`, but since `cliTools` and its dependencies aren't in the image's nix
store, the symlinks cannot be created.

=== Conclusion ===

Image builds successfully but is MISSING critical components:
- Chromium browser (required for headless browsing)
- Fontconfig utilities (fc-list, fc-cache)
- Font packages

This is a Nix configuration issue in the OCI image definition. The `cliTools`
buildEnv is declared in `contents` but its closure is not being properly
included in the layered image output.
